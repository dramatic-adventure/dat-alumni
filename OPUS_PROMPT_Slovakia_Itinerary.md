Their guiding spirit is captured by a Vaclav Havel quote displayed on their website: *"People of the theatre who engage their audiences in a dialogue about the dramas of the world of today and the dramas of the human spirit point the way to the future."*

**PASSAGE: Slovakia 2026** runs **July 12 – August 2, 2026**. It is structured in three movements:
- **Movement 1 — Bratislava** (July 12–15): Arrival, orientation, ensemble formation, first workshops

- **Movement 2 — Slovenský Raj** (July 25–28): Nature workshops, solo creative development, Dobšinská Ice Cave, rest and reflection
- **Movement 3 — Final Košice** (July 29 – August 2): Final creative development, full rehearsal, tech, and the ECLECTIC EVENING performance on August 1
